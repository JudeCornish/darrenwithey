Welcome

"In your quest to reach your goals, don’t let disappointments on the way discourage or stop you achieving what you believe in or set out to accomplish"

I am a Licensed England Squash & Racketball Level 3 Advanced Coach and current England Masters International player.

I have experience coaching at all levels of the sport, with individuals and groups of all ages.

Coaching

Encourage the belief in everyone that they can achieve their goals, through a pathway of fun and enjoyment, that rewards their own work ethic and values, if you put the effort in, together we will reach those goals.

A - Aspiring to achieve short & long-term goals together

B - Believe in ourselves

C - Creativity - allowing your identity to evolve and become evident

D - Disciplined - daily and training routine
